Week 17-18

  • 5 Jul 2022

During this phase, our focus is on testing and merging the changes made by all of the MotionInput teams. We are working on merging significant changes to the MI_v3.2 main branch while conducting comprehensive testing to ensure that all features are error-free. This process involves reviewing each team's code and addressing any issues that arise during testing. Additionally, we are also starting the compilation process for teams that are ready to submit their work to the Windows Store. While not all teams are ready to submit, we are starting with any team that is prepared to do so. Our goal is to ensure that all teams are ready for submission and that our product launch is successful. As such, we are following a methodical and strategic approach to ensure that our product is of the highest quality and meets the expectations of our users.

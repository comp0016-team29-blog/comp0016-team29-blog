During this week, we are still working with Alex Clarke to migrate and test the Mediapipe library. Our goal is to ensure that these changes do not affect the other MotionInput products, and that they function as intended. Additionally, we are creating repositories for individuals, which can then be used by all teams to create their own separate repositories. This is an important step to ensure that all teams can work independently while maintaining version control and consistency across the project.

Furthermore, we are also intensely working with teams that require urgent help with their development process. For example, some teams may require additional manpower to assist with their coding process. Although our primary responsibility is to provide guidance on what they should do, we are still helping them directly during the development process to ensure that they meet their deadlines. Our goal is to ensure that all teams receive the necessary support and resources they need to complete their work and that the overall project is completed successfully.
